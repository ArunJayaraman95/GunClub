Ranges

There is no shooting before 10am or after 9pm, Monday through Saturday, and no shooting before 10am or after 6pm on Sundays, to comply with local ordinances.

Pistol Range

Our outdoor 25-yard pistol range is perfect for handguns or small-caliber rifles.

  • Mon–Sat: 10am–9pm
  • Sun: 10am–6pm

Rifle Range

Experience our 200-yard rifle range with multiple lanes for precision shooting and competitions.

  • Mon–Sat: 10am–9pm
  • Sun: 10am–6pm

Trap & Skeet Range

Join us at our clay target trap range for sporting clays and skeet shooting events.

  • Tuesday: 10am–1pm
  • Wednesday: 4:30pm–9pm
  • Sunday: 10am–1pm
